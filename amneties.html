Our World–Class Facilities

Explore the exceptional amenities and unparalleled services offered at Cosmos Club, designed to cater to every need for leisure, business, and recreation.

Sports & Recreation

Our state-of-the-art complex features two wooden indoor shuttle courts with professional-grade lighting, perfect for casual and competitive play.
Enjoy our 25M x 10M indoor swimming pool, ideal for year-round use and regular training sessions.
The club offers well-maintained basketball and volleyball courts for members to enjoy outdoor recreation and friendly matches.
Members can access a fully equipped health club and a cozy cards room for relaxation and leisure.
Engage in classic games such as billiards, carrom, and cards — perfect for socializing and winding down.
Regular coaching camps are conducted for badminton, roller skating, and swimming under professional supervision.
Our expansive 20,000 sq.ft amphitheatre serves as a vibrant hub for cultural events, performances, and celebrations.
A peaceful reading room equipped with daily newspapers, magazines, and periodicals for our members’ enrichment.

Dining & Accommodation

Restaurant

Indulge in a culinary journey at our multi-cuisine restaurant, offering exquisite dishes prepared by our master chefs.

Permit Room

Unwind with a selection of fine spirits and cocktails in the sophisticated ambiance of our exclusive Permit Room.

Guest House

Comfortable and elegant accommodation for your guests, with Deluxe AC and Suite rooms available.

Multi-Purpose AC Hall

Meeting Halls

Party Areas

Rental Facilities

Various facilities and equipment available for rent to make your event a success.

Parking & Ambiance

Parking Facility
