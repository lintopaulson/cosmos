OUR JOURNEY

Discover Our Story

Established in 1995 and registered under the Charitable Society Act (Reg. No. 159/95), the Cosmos Club is recognized as one of Kerala's finest social destinations. Located conveniently in the heart of Chalakudy , the club spans a generous 3-acre area directly adjacent to the National Highway 544 (NH-544) , ensuring superb accessibility.

OUR FOUNDATION

Mission & Values

We are dedicated to the mental and physical welfare of our members and the general public, without distinction of caste, creed, or religion. A key focus is to render social, moral, and financial assistance to promote National integrity and Communal harmony .

Our Mission

Our core mission is to promote social life by providing a common ground for all communities to meet and fostering a spirit of goodwill and fellowship .

Collaboration

We believe in the power of teamwork and mutual support.

Innovation

We encourage creative thinking and the pursuit of new ideas.

Inclusivity

We welcome members from all backgrounds and perspectives.

Impact

We aim to create positive change through our projects.
