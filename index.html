Cosmos Club is located in the heart of Chalakudy, just a short drive from major city hubs and easily accessible from the airport. Spread across expansive, lush landscapes, the club offers top-notch amenities and hosts a variety of events throughout the year, making it one of the premier family and recreational clubs in the region.
